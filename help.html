Help Center

Welcome to the Friendora.space Help Center! This page is your comprehensive guide to using our platform, resolving issues, and understanding our features. Whether you’re new to the site or a seasoned user, we’re here to ensure your experience is smooth and enjoyable.

About This Help Center

The Help Center is designed to provide step-by-step assistance, answer common questions, and offer solutions to technical or account-related problems. Built by the Friendora.space team, led by Daksh Hande and Aayush Tiwari, this resource reflects our commitment to user support and community engagement. We update it regularly based on user feedback and platform changes.

Getting Started

New to Friendora.space? Here’s how to dive in:
- Sign Up: Click “Sign Up” on the homepage, enter your email and a strong password, then verify your account via the confirmation email.
- Set Up Your Profile: Add your name, a profile picture, and details like hobbies or location to connect with others.
- Start Exploring: Use the search bar to find friends, check the Feed for updates, or join discussions on the Q&A page.

Using Key Features

Here’s how to make the most of Friendora.space:
- Friend Search: Type a name or keyword in the homepage search bar to find people.
- Posting Updates: Visit the Feed, click the post box, write your message (up to 500 characters), and submit.
- Q&A Participation: Ask or answer questions on the Q&A page to engage with the community.
Note: Features like username editing or account deletion are not yet available.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Running into problems? Try these fixes:
- Can’t Log In? Double-check your email and password. Use “Forgot Password” to reset if needed.
- Page Not Loading? Clear your browser cache, switch browsers, or test your internet speed.
- Post Missing? Ensure it meets our Terms (no offensive content), then refresh the Feed.
- Search Not Working? Try broader terms or check your spelling.
Still stuck? Email us at support@friendora.space.

Account Management

Managing your account is simple, with some limitations:
- Profile Updates: Edit your bio or photo anytime from your profile settings.
- Username/Password Changes: Not currently supported; contact support for help.
- Account Deletion: Manual deletion isn’t an option yet—email us to request it.

Support Channels

We’re here to assist you through:
- Email Support: Reach out to support@friendora.space for personalized help (response within 24–48 hours).
- FAQ Page: Visit FAQ for quick answers.
- Q&A Community: Get tips from other users in the Q&A section.

Submitting Feedback

We value your input! Share suggestions, report bugs, or request features by emailing feedback@friendora.space. Your feedback helps us improve Friendora.space for everyone.

Our Promise to You

Friendora.space is built to connect people in a safe, friendly environment. This Help Center is part of our promise to support you every step of the way. If you need assistance beyond what’s here, don’t hesitate to reach out—we’re just an email away!
