Privacy Policy

At Friendora.space, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. By using our website, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details like your name, email address, and profile information when you sign up or update your account.

2. How We Collect Data

Data is collected through forms you fill out (e.g., registration), posts you share, and automatically via cookies and analytics tools.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your information to provide services, personalize your experience, and improve Friendora.space’s functionality.

4. Data Usage

Your data helps us facilitate friend connections, display posts on the Feed, and enable Q&A interactions.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. It may be shared with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting services) under strict confidentiality agreements.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience, track usage patterns, and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We employ SSL encryption, password hashing, and firewalls to protect your data, though no system is 100% secure.

8. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data by contacting support@friendora.space.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to comply with legal obligations.

10. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to external websites. We’re not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Children’s Privacy

Friendora.space is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored on servers outside your country, subject to applicable laws like GDPR or CCPA where relevant.

13. Legal Compliance

We comply with the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, for data handling.

14. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here, and continued use of the site implies acceptance.

15. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, email us at support@friendora.space. We’re here to assist you!
