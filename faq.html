Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Got questions about Friendora.space? We’ve compiled answers to some of the most common queries to help you navigate our platform with ease. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to contact us at support@friendora.space.

1. What is Friendora.space?

Friendora.space is a social networking platform designed to connect people, foster friendships, and allow users to share updates and engage in discussions. It was created by Daksh Hande with the vision of building a simple, friendly online community.

2. How do I create an account?

Visit the homepage, click “Sign Up,” and provide a valid email address and a secure password. Once registered, you can set up your profile with personal details and a photo.

3. How can I find friends on Friendora.space?

Use the search bar on the homepage. Enter a name or keyword, and browse the results to connect with friends.

4. Can I edit my username or password?

Currently, editing your username or changing your password is not available. We’re working on adding these features. For now, contact support for assistance.

5. How do I post an update?

Go to the Feed page, locate the post box, type your message, and click “Submit” to share it with your friends.

6. Why can’t I delete my account?

Manual account deletion isn’t an option yet. If you wish to delete your account, please email support@friendora.space, and we’ll assist you.

7. Is my data secure on Friendora.space?

Yes, we use SSL encryption, password hashing, and other security measures to protect your data. However, we recommend using a strong, unique password for added safety.

8. What can I do on the Q&A page?

The Q&A page lets you ask questions, answer others, and engage in discussions with the Friendora.space community.

9. Why isn’t my post showing up?

Ensure your content follows our Terms of Use. If it’s compliant, try refreshing the page. Contact support if the issue persists.

10. How do I contact support?

Email us at support@friendora.space. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
