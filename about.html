About Friendora.space

Friendora.space is a social networking platform designed to connect people, foster friendships, and share updates in a simple and user-friendly environment. Built from scratch by our CEO, Daksh Hande, using his expertise in web development, this platform aims to bring people closer together.

Why We Built Friendora.space

The idea behind Friendora.space was to create a space where users can easily find and connect with friends, share their thoughts, and stay updated with the latest posts. Daksh Hande envisioned a platform that prioritizes simplicity and meaningful interactions over complexity.

Vision for Friendora.space

Our vision is to create a platform where users can effortlessly search for friends from the homepage, engage in Q&A discussions, and stay updated via the Feed page. We aim to expand features while keeping the user experience intuitive and fun.

Current Features

  • Search for friends directly from the homepage.
  • Create a profile by adding details about yourself.
  • Post updates and see the latest posts on the Feed page.
  • Participate in discussions on the Q&A page.

Features Users Don’t Have

  • Users cannot delete their accounts manually.
  • Editing usernames or changing passwords is not available yet.

User Dos and Don’ts

Do:

  • Create a profile with accurate details about yourself.
  • Search for friends and connect with them.
  • Post updates and engage in Q&A discussions.

Don’t:

  • Share false information on your profile.
  • Attempt to manually delete your account (contact support instead).
  • Expect to edit your username or password at this time.
